Welcome to the Complete Agentic Readiness Assessment

A Two-Phase Framework for Evaluating AI Agent Deployment Readiness

üéØ What is This Assessment?

This assessment helps organizations make TWO critical decisions before deploying autonomous AI agents:

  1. SHOULD we build this agent? (Phase 0: Economic & Process Validation)
  2. CAN we govern it responsibly? (Phase 1: GESR Operational Readiness)

Assessment Structure

PHASE 0: Economic & Process Validation (NEW)

12 questions | 48 points maximum | ~15 minutes

Validates that:

  • The manual process delivers documented ROI
  • The process is mature and stable enough to automate
  • Your organization has discipline to manage the investment

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL: You must score 40+ points with NO red flags to proceed to Phase 1

PHASE 1: GESR Operational Readiness

32 questions | 64 points maximum | ~30 minutes

Evaluates your readiness across four dimensions:

  • Governance: Who controls this agent and how?
  • Ethics: Is this agent aligned with values?
  • Safety: What could go wrong?
  • Responsibility: Who answers when things fail?

Note: Only accessible after passing Phase 0

How to Use This Assessment

  1. Assemble Your Team (90-minute session recommended):
    • Business owners & process stakeholders
    • AI/ML technical teams
    • Finance representative
    • Security & compliance
    • Ethics or Responsible AI functions
  2. Complete Phase 0 - Be honest about process maturity and economics
  3. Review Decision Gate - Assess whether to proceed
  4. Complete Phase 1 (if Phase 0 passes) - Evaluate operational readiness
  5. Review Dashboard - See overall readiness and recommendations
  6. Create Action Plan - Document next steps

Scoring Philosophy

Phase 0 uses 0-4 scale:

  • 4: Excellent - well-established and proven
  • 3: Good - mostly in place
  • 2: Fair - partially implemented
  • 1: Poor - minimal implementation
  • 0: None - does not exist

Phase 1 uses 0-2 scale:

  • 2: YES - fully in place
  • 1: PARTIAL - exists but incomplete
  • 0: NO - does not exist

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

  • Be honest - Finding gaps is the goal, not achieving a perfect score
  • Red flags matter - Certain gaps automatically disqualify proceeding, regardless of overall score
  • This is a conversation starter - Not a certification or compliance requirement
  • Reassess regularly - Every 90 days as capabilities and risks evolve
